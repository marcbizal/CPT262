Bootstrap is the number one web development frame work. Because so many developers use it there's a large base of developers working on templates and plug-ins to make web development faster and easier than ever.

Easy to maintain

Joining a web development project long after it started or taking over a project started by others can be a nightmare of trying to figure out why they built it the way they did. With bootstrap you already know the framework and just have to see where they made changes. When joining a company that uses bootstrap you are ready to work on day one, without a long learning curve to understand they way they do development.
